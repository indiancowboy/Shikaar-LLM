# Wild Boar Breakfast Pizza

## Description
When the craving for pizza meets the call for breakfast, this dish brings the best of both worlds together. A base of sharp cheddar-topped dough bakes until crisp, then gets layered with creamy country-style gravy made from wild boar breakfast sausage. Cracked eggs finish cooking right on top, their yolks still runny for richness. A final hit of lemon zest cuts through the indulgence, creating a pizza that’s hearty, playful, and perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients
1 ball pizza dough
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese 
1 pound wild boar breakfast sausage (or deer country sausage)
2 tablespoons flour
2 cups whole milk
Salt and pepper, to taste
1 teaspoon Aleppo pepper (optional)
4 eggs
Zest of 1 lemon
Preparation
Preheat oven to 500–550°F. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a baking stone or sheet. Top with shredded cheddar and bake until nearly, but not fully, cooked through.
Meanwhile, prepare the gravy. Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium heat. dd the sausage and cook until about 90% done. Stir in the flour and cook for 1–2 minutes to form a roux, then gradually whisk in the milk. Simmer, stirring often, until thick and creamy. Season with salt, pepper, and Aleppo pepper if using.
Remove the pizza from the oven and spoon the sausage gravy evenly over the top. Crack the eggs directly onto the pizza, spacing them out so each slice will hold some yolk. Return the pizza to the oven and bake until the whites are set but the yolks remain runny, about 4–6 minutes.
 with fresh lemon zest. Slice and serve immediately, enjoying the blend of molten cheese, savory gravy, and rich egg yolk on crisp crust.
