# Indian-Spiced Yellowtail Ceviche

## Description
Ceviche is a dish built on brightness and simplicity, and this version adds an Indian twist with garam masala. Fresh yellowtail is cured in lime juice, then tossed with crisp vegetables, green mango, and herbs for layers of texture and flavor. The spices bring warmth to the sharp acidity, making this fusion ceviche perfect for scooping up with your favorite chips or crackers.

Ingredients
1 pound yellowtail (or other firm white fish), diced small
2 shallots
2 green onions
2  chiles
1/2 cucumber
1/4 cup fresh cilantro
Juice of 4 limes
2 tablespoons garam masala
1 tablespoon salt
Chips or crackers, for serving

Preparation
In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced yellowtail, shallots, green onions, chilies, cucumber, and cilantro. Pour the lime juice and stir well to coat. Add the garam masala and salt, until evenly seasoned. Cover and refrigerate for  to allow the fish to cure in the lime juice and the flavors to meld.
Serve chilled with chips or crackers for scooping.
