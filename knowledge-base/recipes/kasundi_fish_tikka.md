# Kasundi Fish Tikka

## Description
This Bengali classic is all about bold mustard flavor balanced with creamy yogurt and aromatic spices. Traditionally made with freshwater fish and kasundi—a pungent, fermented mustard found in Indian markets—it’s a dish that celebrates the flavors of eastern India.




Ingredients


1/2 cup plain yogurt
2 tablespoons kasundi mustard
1 tablespoon garam masala
1 teaspoon turmeric
1/2 tablespoon salt



1/4 cup plain yogurt 

1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger

1 tablespoon tamarind paste
6–8 curry leaves, chopped



1 red onion, thinly sliced
Juice of 1 lemon
Black pepper, to taste
Roti, for serving
