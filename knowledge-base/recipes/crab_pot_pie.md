# Crab Pot Pie

## Description
This recipe takes the familiar comfort of a pot pie and layers it with the bold, aromatic flavors of Indian cooking. Sweet Dungeness crab is folded into a filling infused with mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, and Kashmiri chilies, then tucked beneath a flaky biscuit crust. It’s a dish that bridges coastal seafood traditions with the warmth of spiced comfort food—hearty, fragrant, and unique.
