## Overview

Punjab, the “land of five rivers,” is India’s agricultural heartland and home of some of its richest, most robust food. Trade routes and migrations shaped a cuisine that blends Hindu, Sikh, and Pathan influences and became famous across India through **dhabas** (roadside eateries).

Punjab’s most iconic contributions are the **tandoor** (for smoky breads and meats), **dal makhani** (slow-cooked black lentils), and **butter chicken** (created from leftover tandoori chicken simmered in tomato-butter gravy).

## Core Flavor Building Blocks

- **Aromatics**: onion, garlic, ginger, tomato, green chilli, fenugreek leaves, whole garam masala.
- **Dairy & fats**: ghee, butter, cream, yoghurt, paneer.
- **Spices**: garam masala, cumin, coriander, fenugreek, Kashmiri chilli.
- **Grains**: wheat rotis and parathas, makki di roti with mustard greens.
- **Souring**: tomato and yoghurt.

## Techniques

- **Tandoor cooking** for breads, kebabs, and smoky meats.
- **Bhuna-onion–tomato masalas** cooked down until deep and sweet.
- **Slow-cooking** of dals (e.g. dal makhani) and meat curries.
- **Dhaba-style high-heat cooking** with lots of ghee or butter.

## Protein Pairing (Shikaar)

- **Fish**: tandoori fish tikka, pan-fried in tomato-onion masala, butter-masala fish.
- **Venison**: tandoori venison tikka, mustard-ghee fry, tomato-garlic curry for shoulder cuts.
- **Wild boar**: rich tomato-onion masala, tandoori boar kebabs, slow-cooked boar in buttery gravy.
- **Duck/goose**: butter masala or robust tomato curries.

## Pattern Blocks

- **Punjabi tandoori base**: yoghurt, ginger-garlic, chilli, garam masala, fenugreek leaves, lemon, oil → grill or roast.
- **Dhaba masala fry**: onion–tomato–ginger–garlic–chilli cooked in ghee, then protein added and reduced.
- **Butter masala base**: butter, tomato purée, ginger-garlic, garam masala, cream, fenugreek.

## When Shikaar Should Recommend Punjab

- User wants **smoky, grilled, tandoori** flavours.
- User asks for **butter chicken–style** or **tomato-butter curries**.
- User has **oily fish, venison, boar** and wants **bold, hearty, dhaba-style food**.
